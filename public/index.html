SIMPLE FUNERAL PLANNER

Funeral costs have doubled over the last 12 years. But there are simple options to help plan a meaningful and affordable send off for you or a loved one. This easy planning tool shows some of the simple options available, gives average costs in your area, and helps you prepare to discuss your needs with family, friends or a funeral director.

Facing an immediate funeral and struggling to pay? Contact Down to Earth to see how we can help.

Direct cremation

The simplest and most affordable option, and growing in popularity. With no funeral service, there is choice and flexibility about how you say farewell.

Simple funeral plan

A more traditional funeral service, but without the bells and whistles. This is a good option when you want a full funeral service, but at a more modest cost.

Simple bespoke plan

Want something more than a simple service? There are several options to make a simple plan tailored to your needs.
