What’s a simple bespoke funeral service?

Funeral directors offer many extra services and products in addition to a simple funeral package.

These all come with additional costs, so compare itemised quotes from several funeral directors.

Although these added services may be convenient and useful, remember that you can arrange them yourself if you need to reduce costs. In addition to the funeral service, examples of additional products offered include:
  • Flowers
  • Death / funeral notice
  • Limousines
  • Orders of service
  • Venue hire
  • Catering
Brian's Story
"Brian organised his brother’s funeral, and for several reasons opted for more than a basic service. He and his brother lived in the family home their whole life so, despite the higher cost, it was important to him that the hearse left directly from the house.
Brian is quite frail and doesn’t drive so chose the optional limousine service for himself and other members of the family. As people travelled a long way to attend the funeral he decided to opt for a service time later in the day, which was more convenient but costlier than an early morning service.

OPTIONS TO TAILOR YOUR FUNERAL SERVICE

Select items below to add to your plan your plan. Note the tips for reducing these costs.

Order Sheets Tip: To save money you could ask family or friends to design and print them.
Funeral Notice Tip: Instead of a paid notice, post the details on social media.
Flowers Tip: Reduce costs by providing your own, or ask attendees to donate to charity rather than buying flowers
Venue Hire Tip: Having a gathering at home could save a lot of money.
Catering Tip: Avoid expensive catering by doing it yourself with contributions from family and friends.
Limousine Tip: Save money by using a personal car or taxi, meeting the hearse at the crematorium or cemetery.
